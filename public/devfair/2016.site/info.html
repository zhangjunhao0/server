We got tired of waiting in line.

In response to the failiures of DECAF, the Computer Science and Engineering Society decided to do something about the way we run job fairs at UCSD. No more waiting in line, no more wasted resumes, no more talking to employers who "just aren't interested right now". And so we took a good hard look at ourselves and *cough*MIT*cough* and decided to reverse the whole process. At this job fair, the students are the ones in control: the employers walk around and get to talk to who they want to talk to. We will change this later.
